GIIS Circular

This web application was designed for the final year project as a part of my undergraduate curriculum. It aims to bridge the communication gap between parents and the school authorities. The authorities can post circulars and notices, whereas parents can directly open communication on a specific circular posted on the website. Status of the communication is also recorded to ensure every query is resolved in due time. SQLite database is used for storing circulars, staff and children data, and the messages exchanged between them along with their status.
